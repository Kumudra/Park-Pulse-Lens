Waste & Recycling Practices

Current systems, common mistakes, and successful programs in Myanmar

Current Practices

  • National Strategy (2018–2030)

    Framework for waste management improvement with slow implementation

  • Municipal Practices

    Reliance on open dumping and landfills with little separation

  • Informal Recycling

    Junk shops and collectors play major role without standardized system

Common Mistakes

  • Mixing Waste

    Households mix recyclables with general waste, complicating recycling

  • Burning Trash

    Leads to air pollution and health risks in communities

  • Low Awareness

    Misconceptions about recycling methods and benefits
