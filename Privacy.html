Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you engage with Park Pulse Lens community initiatives.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

Transparent information about how we handle your data

Minimal Data

We collect only essential information needed for park revitalization participation and community engagement.

Secure Storage

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures and encryption protocols.

Your Control

You have full rights to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time.

Our Privacy Commitment

Detailed explanation of our data practices

We collect information that helps us coordinate park revitalization efforts effectively:

  • Volunteer Information: Name, email, phone number for event coordination
  • Participation Details: Event preferences, availability, skills
  • Community Feedback: Suggestions and comments about park improvements
  • Technical Data: Browser information, IP address (for security purposes)
  • Location Data: General area information for local park initiatives

We never collect sensitive personal information unless absolutely necessary for safety reasons.

Your information helps us build stronger park communities:

Purpose Examples
Event Coordination Sending reminders, updates, and safety information for park cleanups
Community Building Connecting volunteers with similar interests and skills
Impact Reporting Anonymous statistics on park improvements and volunteer hours
Communication Newsletters about upcoming projects and success stories
Safety & Security Emergency contact information and event safety planning

We believe in transparency about who sees your information:

With Your Consent
  • Local park authorities for coordination
  • Community partners for collaborative events
  • Sponsors (only aggregated, anonymous data)
Legal Requirements
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety of our community
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activities

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

We maintain your data with care and responsibility:

Retention Period

We retain your information for as long as necessary for park community engagement, or until you request deletion. Inactive accounts are automatically deleted after 2 years.

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Employee privacy training

You have control over your personal information:

Access Right

View your data anytime

Correction

Update inaccurate information

Deletion

Request data removal

Opt-Out

Stop communications

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@parkpulselens.org. We typically respond within 7 business days.

Children's Privacy

Park Pulse Lens is committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Children's participation in park events requires parental consent
  • Parents can review, edit, or delete their child's information
  • Special safety protocols for youth volunteer activities

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal, essential technology to improve your experience:

Essential Cookies
  • Session cookies for login functionality
  • Security cookies for protection
  • Preference cookies for language settings
Optional Cookies
  • Analytics cookies (anonymous usage data)
  • Marketing cookies (with your consent)

Cookie Control: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.
